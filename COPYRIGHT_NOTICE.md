## Long-Term Planning

The system supports comprehensive long-term transformation plans:
- 3-month plans
- 6-month plans
- 1-year plans
- 2-year plans
- 5-year plans
- 10-year plans

Plans are generated with difficulty levels based on urgency:
- Gentle (Low urgency)
- Moderate (Medium urgency)
- Aggressive (High urgency)
- Extreme (Critical urgency)

## Medical Specialties

The system includes comprehensive medical specialty analyses:
- Radiology (X-Ray, CT, MRI, Ultrasound, PET, SPECT, Nuclear Medicine)
- Cardiology (ECG, Echocardiogram, Stress Tests, Cardiac Catheterization, Holter Monitor, Cardiac MRI)
- Nuclear Medicine (Bone Scans, PET/SPECT, Thyroid Scans, Cardiac Perfusion, etc.)
- Neurology (EEG, EMG, Nerve Conduction Studies)
- Pulmonology (Spirometry, Lung Volumes, Diffusion Capacity, ABG)
- Gastroenterology (Endoscopy, Colonoscopy, Manometry, pH Monitoring)
